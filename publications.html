My relationship with my dad was complicated. In early 2020, he passed away due to complication from COVID-19. This chapbook explores the connections between us, who he was, and who I am because of him. I spent over a decade in the Southwestern United States. For school. Work. A failing romance. This chapbook is about those years, their aftermath, and how we remember those who harm us.
