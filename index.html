Kris Hiles

About PublicationsLetters

Kris Hiles is an autistic, lesbian author and artist. Born and raised in Pennsylvania, she spent over a decade in the Southwestern United States before returning to New England. She now lives near the coast with her wife, Nel, and their collection of plants and vinyl records.

Her writing has appeared in a number of online publications, and she previously edited GLITCHWORDS.

When not busy, she can frequently be found in museums, rock hunting, at a baseball game, or cooking.

Connect with her on Twitter @KrisHiles.
